Welcome to my Art History Quiz!

This site hopes to help you learn some basic information about various painting movements through history! You'll be able to see some art, meet some artists, and learn what truly separates each into the movements they are associated with. Plus, as the name implis, you'll be able to test your knowledge to identify the movement based on the painting or artist! Additionally, the color design of this site is inspired by historical painting colors.
